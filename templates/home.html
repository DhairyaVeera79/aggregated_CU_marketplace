About Us

The CU Boulder Marketplace is a dedicated platform for students to buy and sell items within the university community. Whether you're looking to purchase textbooks, furniture, electronics, or any other essentials, our marketplace connects you with fellow students, ensuring trustworthy transactions and fostering a supportive community.

Our mission is to provide a seamless and efficient way for students to exchange goods and services, promoting sustainability and affordability. Join us in building a vibrant and interconnected campus economy!
